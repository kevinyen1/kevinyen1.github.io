F.A.Q.
Q: What is Irvine famous for?
Being "the only college town in world with no bars open past dinner time, a slice of master-planned hell, where cars built before 1990 will be ticketed just for parking overnight outside your own home."
Q: What type of amenities does the city provide?
State-of-the-art security. You can walk outside at 2AM and not get kidnapped. If you're under 18, you might get busted for the 11PM curfew though. But seriously. We've pumped "hundreds of millions of dollars on a Great Park that its founders still claim would rival Central Park, and all they have to show for it is an ugly-ass [orange] balloon."
Q: Seriously though, give me some history!
As one of the largest planned cities in the nation, Irvine is known for its meticulously planned communities. Relatively new, the modern city of Irvine is actually less than 50 years old!
Q: What's your favorite activity in Irvine?
Driving. Sunroof pulled all the way back, music blasting. I love the feel of the open road zooming underneath me.
